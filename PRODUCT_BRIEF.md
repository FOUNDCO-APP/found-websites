## THE ONE-LINE PITCH

Answer a few questions. See your website. Get found online — from your phone, in under 10 minutes, for less than $40/month.

---

## THE PROBLEM

Small service businesses — contractors, painters, roofers, groomers, event companies, landscapers — share the same three painful problems:

1. **Their website is embarrassing or doesn't exist.** Wix and Squarespace require too much work. The owner still has to be a designer, a copywriter, and a web developer. They don't have time. The result: a half-finished site or no site at all.

2. **Job site photos are a mess.** Scattered in personal camera rolls, mixed with baby pictures, never organized, never used. Their best work is invisible.

3. **Social media never happens.** Too tired after a long day. Even when they take great photos on the job, turning them into posts is one more thing they don't have time for.

CompanyCam solves #2 but costs too much and ignores #1 and #3.
Squarespace/Wix solve #1 but require real work and never connect to the job site.
Nobody solves all three.

**Found solves all three. From one app.**

---

## THE SOLUTION

A mobile-first app that:
1. **Asks smart onboarding questions** → generates a complete, professional website automatically
2. **Keeps job site photos organized** in the app (off the personal camera roll)
3. **Flags photos for website or social** with one tap — ready when the owner has a moment

The owner never touches a website builder. They never write copy. They never design anything.
They just answer questions and take photos.

---

## THE NON-NEGOTIABLE DESIGN STANDARD

Every Found website must look like Apple built it.

- Clean, minimal, intentional — nothing unnecessary
- Typography-based logo as default (elegant, not cheap) — owner's business name as beautiful type
- Logo upload option for owners who already have one
- Jony Ive-inspired color palettes as presets
- Custom hex color option for owners who already have brand colors
- Mobile first — these owners are on job sites, not at desks
- If it needs a tutorial, it's too complicated

The goal: the owner sees their generated site and says "this looks better than I ever imagined."
They feel proud. They feel big. They feel like they belong next to any large company.

## ONBOARDING FLOW

Owner answers questions → site is generated → owner approves → site is live.

The owner never sees the technical side. They have a business to run.

**Question categories (to be finalized with the team):**
- Business name
- Industry / type of work
- City / service area

## CORE FEATURES

### 1. Auto-Generated Website
- Driven by onboarding answers
- Looks Apple-quality — not a template, not cookie-cutter
- Typography logo if no logo provided
- Pages: Home, Services, Contact/Estimate (Phase 1)
- Upgrades unlock more pages and features (see below)

### 2. In-App Camera System
- Photos and videos captured inside Found — never mixed with personal camera roll
- Organized by project/job automatically
- Workers upload → admin reviews → admin decides what gets used

### 3. Two-Flag Curation
Every photo gets zero, one, or both flags:
- ❤️ **Website Heart** — photo appears in the website gallery + online content
- ⭐ **Social Star** — photo queued in the social media prep area

These are independent. A photo can be both, one, or neither.

### 4. Social Media Prep
- Social-starred photos collected in one place
- Sized and formatted for Instagram, Facebook, etc.
- Owner saves to camera roll or exports when ready
- No auto-posting — simple, no API approval hell

### 5. Website Gallery Auto-Sync
- Every ❤️-hearted photo automatically appears in the website gallery
- No separate upload. No second step. It just shows up.

## UPGRADE / PREMIUM FEATURES

Base plan includes the website + camera + two-flag system.
Upgrades are add-ons the owner can purchase at any time.

| Upgrade | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **Shopping Cart** | Stripe-powered product listings for owners who sell physical goods or services |
| **Estimates & Quotes** | Create, send, and track estimates — owner builds a quote, client approves it |
| **Shared Gallery Link** | Shareable link the owner sends to a client showing their project in progress |
| **Custom Domain** | Connect their own domain (e.g., barriobuilders.com) instead of [slug].foundco.app |
| **Additional Pages** | Blog, FAQ, Team page, Service area pages |
| **Human Design Session** | One-time upgrade — real designer reviews and customizes their site |
