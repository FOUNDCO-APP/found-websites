## THE VISION (Shawn's words)

Fill a missing gap in the market. Small business owners on a budget — no time, no money to hire a developer or do it themselves. Wix/Squarespace are broken because the owner still has to do too much work.

**Found solves this: answer onboarding questions → site is generated for you. Done.**

No technical knowledge required. Owner never sees the backend. They have a business to run.

- Owner answers a set of questions (team already designed these in a prior session — need to recreate)

## CORE FEATURES

### 1. Camera System (in-app)
- Capture photos inside the app — never uses personal camera roll
- Two flags per photo:
  - ❤️ Heart — flags for website gallery / online shopping cart
  - ⭐ Star — flags for social media prep
- When they have a break or get home, flagged photos are ready to use

### 2. Auto-Generated Website
- Looks like Apple designed it — NOT cookie cutter, NOT cheap
- Typography logo auto-generated from business name (for owners without a logo)
- Logo upload option if they have one
- Color palette: Jony Ive / Apple-inspired presets
- Custom color option if they already have brand colors
- Goal: owner feels proud — looks as big as Apple

### 3. Social Media Prep
- Starred photos collected and sized/formatted for Instagram, Facebook, etc.
- Ready to post when they have time — NOT auto-posted

### 4. Website Gallery
- Hearted photos auto-appear in website gallery
- Can also link to shopping cart (upgrade)
